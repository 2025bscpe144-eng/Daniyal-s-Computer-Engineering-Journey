My name is Daniyal and I am a Computer Engineering student at UET Lahore, Faisalabad Campus. This blog documents my university experience — honestly, week by week — from the nervousness of Day 1 to the pride of a department exhibition presentation.

I started this blog on the suggestion of **Dr. Bilal Ahmad**, my professor at UET Faisalabad, who encouraged students to leave a digital footprint — to document their learning journey so that both they and others can benefit from it. You can explore his research work on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com.au/citations?user=8nZ0jVkAAAAJ&hl=en).
